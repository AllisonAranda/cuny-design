    Baruch College

    Graphic Communication

    Degree: B.A.

    Department of Fine and Performing Arts

    As a Specialization

    Students who wish to study graphic design as a major concentration should refer to the Business Communications major Specialization in Graphic Communication.

    The graphic communication specialization provides training in graphics as well as exposure to the broader aspects of business communication. It prepares the graphic artist to enter a business environment.

    Consult the online Bulletin for information about course requirements. Contact the Department of Fine and Performing Arts to meet with a faculty advisor.

    As a Minor

    A Tier III Minor in graphics requires students to complete two graphics courses at or above the 3000-level, and one capstone course, which is at the 4000-level or above.
